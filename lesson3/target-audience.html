Target Audience

This site is focus in the audience that like to go outdoors to enjoy the day inclusing female and male 18 years old and above that are interested in weather information to go outdoor such camping, hiking, fishing, yoga and more outdoors activities.

Personas

  • Persona Male age 18-59 that drive a commercial truck or part of his work need information of weather for safety precautions. David is a traveler around the country his passion is hiking in the most challenge mountain in different states, he has a truck driver job that he must need the weather information due to is crucial for safety.


  • Persona Female age 18 - 59 that enjoy have outdoor actitivies and share with family. Jacky is a yoga instructor that enjoys doing yoga in the woods, one time at week she has a group class to do yoga in the rocky mountain, and for her to be aware of the weather increase the chances to have a successful class.
