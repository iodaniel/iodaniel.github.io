Mobile First - WDD330📱

Importance of Mobile First

  • Mobile traffic is higher that PC
  • Do Mobile First to increase usability and beauty
  • Responsive is not equal to Mobile First
  • Mobile First Design provides best experience across many devices
  • Start small and scale up

Increase user Experience (UX)

  • Be There for the users (Immediate)
  • Be Quick follow the concept "LESS IS MORE"
  • Be Useful the relevancy of your site is for the content
  • Minimize steps increase user experience

Speed Matters

  • Reduce image usage throughout sites/ apps
  • SVG/webfonts are lower band width
  • Optimize anb minify CSS and JS
  • Gzip files to eliminate unnecessary Data

Improve UX Mobile First

  • Put your Navbar and most bottom to increase usability
  • Put menu with border as a button to get +20% more interactivity, avoid use only hamburger menu.
  • ADS: stop being intrusive people visit your website for your contant not for your ads.
  • Never use a popups.
  • Your Content first.

To increase the usability and user experinece (UX) 🚀 is importatn to follow the mobile first and follow the concept of design less is more.
